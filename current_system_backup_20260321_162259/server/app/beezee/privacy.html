Beezee Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Beezee ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information (name, email, location), financial data (transactions, revenue, banking sync), and usage data (device info, app patterns) to provide our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide functionality, generate financial reports, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on consent, contract performance, legal obligation, and our legitimate interests in providing a secure service.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal or financial data. We may share information with trusted service providers or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement end-to-end encryption, secure transmission protocols, and regular security audits to protect your business data at rest and in transit.

7. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on secure servers located in specific African regions (Kenya/South Africa/Nigeria) aligned with our primary mission.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal and financial data. You may also request data portability in a machine-readable format.

9. Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time. Essential service-related notifications will still be sent.

10. Children's Privacy

Beezee is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in Kenya, South Africa, or Nigeria to ensure regional compliance and performance optimization.

12. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced via email or in-app alerts.

14. Regional Compliance

We strictly comply with regional data protection laws, including:

  • South Africa: Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We implement strict controls for the collection and processing of data for South African users.
  • Kenya: Data Protection Act (Kenya).
  • Nigeria: Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

15. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a significant data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery.
